Twitter is a free microblogging social networking service that allows registered users to broadcast short messages or posts called tweets. Users can broadcast Twitter tweets and follow the tweets of other users through the use of a variety of platforms and devices. Tweets and replies to tweets can be sent via text message from a mobile phone, desktop client or through Twitter.com website.

Tweets, which may include hyperlinks are limited to 140 characters. Tweets can be delivered to followers in real time, they can seem like instant messages to the novice user.

By using Twitter you can follow your favourite celebrity, sports person, politician, music artist, etc and find the latest updates about them. You can also post videos and photos on Twitter.
